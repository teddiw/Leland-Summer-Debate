2020 Session: July 20 - 24th

2020 sign-ups will open July 3rd!

The program is run by high school debate students and is for incoming
freshmen from Bret Harte Middle School. Session days will
be held at online from 9 AM to 2 PM. More information about
the video conferencing software we will use will be announced soon.

This camp will teach students about the basics of debate including
constructing arguments, writing cases, and giving speeches. By the end of
the week, students will have to opportunity to apply this knowledge to
participate in two debate rounds.

Because this is a free program and there is restricted space, only a limited
number of sign-ups will be accepted.

Acceptance to this program does not guarantee acceptance to the
Leland Speech and Debate Team.

Visit this website for updates on program activities!
