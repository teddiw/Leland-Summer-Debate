2019 Session: July 15 - 19th

2019 sign-ups are now open!

The program is run by high school debate students and is for incoming
9th graders from Bret Harte Middle School. Session days will
be held at Leland High School from 9 AM-12:30 PM.

This camp will teach students about the basics of debate including
making arguments, writing cases, and giving speeches. By the end of
the week, students will have to opportunity to apply this knowledge to
participate in two debate rounds.

Because this is a free program and there is limited space, only the first
16 sign-ups will be accepted.

Acceptance to this program does not guarantee acceptance to the
Leland Speech and Debate Team.

Visit this website for updates of program activities!
