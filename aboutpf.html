What is Public Forum debate?

Public Forum debate or PF is a partner style debate that focuses on current issues.
This style is the most widespread form of high school debate accros the United States.
Individuals will give short 2-4 minute speeches that are interspersed
with 3 minute "crossfire" sections, questions and answers between opposed debaters.
